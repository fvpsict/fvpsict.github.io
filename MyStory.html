My job evolves managing all Information Technology (ICT) related matters of a Government Primary School. I also ensure that staff and students are aware of cyber safety when online.
I interact with Students, Teachers and Parents

In the course of my work, I get to explore new and exciting trends in the Digital World. An example is starting a Youtube Live Channel for the school for a live prize presentation as gathering in large group in this season of COVID is not possible.
